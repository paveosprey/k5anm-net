**Antenna Projects and Field Notes**

Antenna selection and construction is one of the most impactful decisions an amateur operator makes — a well-matched antenna outperforms expensive radio equipment every time. This section documents antenna builds, field measurements, SWR data, and practical comparisons across HF, VHF, and UHF configurations used by K5ANM. Coverage includes portable antennas suitable for POTA activations, fixed home station setups, and emergency deployments where rapid assembly and multiband capability are priorities.
