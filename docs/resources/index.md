**Downloads, References, and Links**

A curated collection of reference materials, downloadable documents, frequency charts, and external links useful for Travis County amateur radio operators. Includes ICS forms for ARES activations, CHIRP codeplug files, band plans, repeater directories, and links to TCARES, AARC, ARRL, and TDEM resources. Content is organized for quick access during both routine operations and emergency deployments.
